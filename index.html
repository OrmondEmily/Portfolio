About Me

I graduated from UNC Chapel Hill in May of 2025. Here I received a B.S. in Biology with a Second Major in Geography. During my time here I learned mapping skills in ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, ArcGIS Online, R Studio, Adobe Illustrator, and Leaflet. I've also learned how to think spatially, applying my GIS skills projects focusing on community and environmental based problems. Though these projects I worked in a variety of teams focusing on collaboration, however, also learned how to take initative and complete projects independently. I enjoy visualising data and have a very strong attention to detail which combined with my love for art helps me create visually appealing map layouts. I created this website myself using skills I learned in a webmapping course. Click through my website to learn more about the projects and coursework I completed.
