Applying Buffers, Intersects, and Clips

I created this map in QGIS to see where bird conservation sites and wind turbine noise pollution overlap. I created an 10 mile buffer around the wind turbine locations to represent the noise pollution emmited. I then preformed an intersect to see where these areas interfeered with bird conservation zones.
